# About ReadTheMastersAI

ReadTheMastersAI publishes **AI transcriptions and translations** of landmark public-domain
mathematics and physics texts, rendered as clean LaTeX, with the original source always cited.

Our goals:

- Preserve historic works as faithful, reusable LaTeX transcriptions and readable translations.
- Publish **only public-domain texts** — verified by an automated, sourced copyright check that
  applies both the author's-death rule (life + 70) and the US 95-year publication rule.
- Keep the whole corpus **open (CC0)** so it is useful as a dataset, not just a website.

Every text carries a **quality status** — from *AI draft* (machine output, not yet human-checked)
through *spot-checked* and *proofread* to *validated* (independently confirmed) — and records which
AI model produced it. Nothing hides how it was made.

The project is community-powered: contributors run the transcription and translation pipeline on
their **own** AI accounts and submit the results for review. See **[Contribute](/contribute/)**.
